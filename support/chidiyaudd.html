Chidia Uddi

Chidia Uddi is a fun and addictive game inspired by ages old popular indian childhood game.

This is a very challenging game that involves your ability to think on the spot.

Get ready to test your response time to decide whether the picture being displayed on the screen can fly.

How to play:

In this Game, Images will keep coming on your screen one by one.

If the object or character flies, swipe up on the screen.

If it does't then swipe down.
