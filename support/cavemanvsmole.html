Caveman vs Mole

Caveman vs Mole is a very classic hamster game, test your hand speed and vision, come and challenge it!

This behavior is nasty!

The caveman must teach them a lesson!

They dig holes, and find everything they can to feed themselves.

The caveman are very angry with these moles, and will kill all moles we can find!

Use a hammer and hit those moles when they run out the hole and steal vegetables.
