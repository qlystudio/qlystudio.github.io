Don't Shoot Me

Don't Shoot Me is a free and funny shooting game.

It's different from other shooting games.

Your mission is not to shoot bird but to control the bird to avoid being shot by the arrow.

How to play:

• Flick left or right to control the bird move left or right.

• Be careful that each arrow speed is different.
