Color Bounce Ball

Color Bounce Ball is a simple but addictive game.

You need to control the ball jump over blocks. Keep the ball bouncing by matching the color with the blocks. If the color of the ball is mismatched with the color of the bar then its over.

How to play:

- Tap the color of the next block to jump to it.

- Tap or collide with the rainbow powerup to become invincible for 7 seconds and gain double the poins.
