Candy Demon

Candy Demon is a free to play addictive survival game. A simple left-right touch control based game. It is easy and fun to play.

How to play：

- The Candy Demon's life will decrease with time.

- Tap left or right to controll the Candy Demon eat candies to increase his life.

- Watch out for the blocks!
