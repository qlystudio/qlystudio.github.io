Catch Falling Eggs

Catch Falling Eggs is one of the most exciting game and its totally free.

You must be quick enough to catch eggs as much as you can. If the basket is full you can fry eggs to reduce the number of eggs. Don't catch the bomb. You can't crush more then four eggs.

How to play:

- Catch the eggs with the basket.

- Swipe screen to move the basket.

- Don't catch the bomb.

- Tap the pan to cook.
