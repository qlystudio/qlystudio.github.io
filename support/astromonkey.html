Super Astro Monkey

Super Astro Monkey is a simple but addictive reaction-based fast paced action game.

Your mission is to play through infinite levels of space, collecting bananas and avoiding astroids as they come at you faster and faster.

This challenging and super fun game requires the player to be very fast and alert in order to succeed and score as many points as possible.
