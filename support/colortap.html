Tap Color Tap

Tap Color Tap is an addictive brain training game against the clock.

Tap Color Tap is a healthy exercise for your brain to help recognize and match patterns and increase memory management ability.

You are given a reference color each level and you are supposed to tap all the colors the same as the reference color as fast as you can before the timer runs out.

Are you ready for the game that will blow you mind? Prepare your fingers because there will be a lot of tapping!
