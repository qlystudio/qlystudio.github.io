Astro Trek Adventure

Astro Trek Adventure is an arcade game in which the player navigates a starship through the moving asteroids and collect stars.

How to play:

- Pan left side of the screen to control the ship.

- Avoid asteroids, black holes and bombs.

- Collect stars as many as you can.
