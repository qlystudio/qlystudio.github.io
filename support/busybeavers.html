The Busy Beavers

The Busy Beavers is a Frogger-like game where you help Mr. Beaver cross the river to collect logs for his dam.

How to play:

- Swipe to move

- Move across platforms to reach the other and of the water

- Run over twigs to gather them

- Gather 8 twigs before time expires to receive a time bonus
